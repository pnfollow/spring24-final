The reason the planets are in the order they are is thanks to the Sun's solar wind. The rocky planets are more dense, therefore they were not pushed out as far as the gas planets. Pluto, despite not being a gas planet but an 'ice' planet, is further out from it's placement inside the Kuiper belt, which is why it was declassified to a dwarf planet."

Pluto

Pluto is no longer considered a planet from it's inability to clear it's orbit, however it met the other classifications to be considered a planet (Having enough mass to maintin a round shape, and an orbit around the sun.)
